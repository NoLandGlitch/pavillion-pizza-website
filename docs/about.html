Our Story

Serving Landisville, New Jersey since 1998.

Serving Landisville Since 1998

Pavillion Ristorante & Pizzeria is a family-owned business that takes pride in our food and our service. Our founders were born and raised in Naples, Italy, and remain committed to serving our community with the best pizza and Italian food we can make.

We believe food brings people together, and that every meal is a chance to create memories with the people you love.

Our Approach to Food

Every pizza starts with a family recipe — fresh, high-quality ingredients, from our homemade sauce to our hand-tossed dough. We use only the freshest ingredients and the best cheese and toppings we can find, because every slice should be a delicious and satisfying experience.

While we honor tradition, we also love to experiment and create new dishes — combining classic flavors with new and exciting ingredients.

Family and Community

We’re proud to be a family-owned part of the Landisville community, and we’re grateful for every neighbor who’s made us part of their routine — whether that’s a weeknight dinner, a family celebration, or an office lunch.
