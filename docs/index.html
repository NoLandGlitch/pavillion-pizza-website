Our Story

Pavillion Ristorante & Pizzeria has been serving Landisville, New Jersey since 1998. We’re a family-owned business, and our founders were born and raised in Naples, Italy — bringing real Italian food traditions to our corner of South Jersey.

From our homemade sauce to our hand-tossed dough, we make everything the way a family recipe should be made: fresh, and with care.
